PureClass Technologies is an IT consultancy founded in 1982. We are customer-focused and results-oriented—we care about your business as if it were our own.

Our core capabilities include:

  • data analysis and reporting
  • data porting and scrubbing
  • customised solutions (standalone and web-based)
  • problems that others put in the “too hard” basket

Some of our projects have included:

  • a money market system for one of the Australia’s largest stockbrokers
  • a bond valuation system incorporating custom mathematical algorithms
  • a live-to-air televised sport tracking and reporting system
  • a live-to-air televised election coverage and forecasting system
  • an operating system emulator to prolong the life of legacy software
  • a Y2K solution that involved disassembling and patching machine code
  • a paperless picking warehouse system handling over 10,000 picks/day
  • an international telephony reporting system handling 3.5m records/month
